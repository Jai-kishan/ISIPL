    About Us

    Who we are

    We Provide Unique & Best Solutions For Industries!

    Industrial Supply International Pty Ltd is a leading distributor and supplier of industrial equipment and parts, serving the Oil & Gas and Mining industries throughout the Australian Pacific region. Backed by extensive experience and a dedicated team, we are committed to delivering high-quality products and consistently exceeding customer expectations.

    Headquartered in Brisbane, Australia, we have a strong presence in the industrial market, offering a diverse range of premium products. Our extensive catalog serves not only the Oil & Gas sector but also a variety of industries including Mining, Telecommunications, Solar, and more.

    We take great pride in our roots and the journey that has established us as a trusted name in the industry. Our team of passionate professionals is committed to providing tailored solutions, ensuring the unique needs of our local and international clients are met with efficiency, reliability, and exceptional service.
